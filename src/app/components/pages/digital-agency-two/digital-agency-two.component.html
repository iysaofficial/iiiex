Indonesia International
Invention Expo

Indonesian young scientist association (IYSA) is the organization that concern to give the opportunities for the young millennial generation to show their competency, talent and passion especially in the field of science and technology. That is why IYSA focuses for conducting some international science activities, one of them is invention and innovation competition. In this moment , IYSA collaborate and cooperate with Politeknik Negeri Semarang to hold INDONESIA INTERNATIONAL INVENTION EXPO (IIIEX). We hope , this event will be a great moment for the millennial generation to improve their skill and ability in the field of science and technology especially in invention and innovation exhibition and competition.

GOLDEN TICKET!!!

Join the IIIEX event organized by the Indonesian Young Scientist Association (IYSA) and Politeknik Negeri Semarang! Here, every high school team has a chance to win a valuable golden ticket that brings a special benefit: a free entry test to enter the POLINES campus.
